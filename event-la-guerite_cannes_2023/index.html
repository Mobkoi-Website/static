An Unforgettable Luncheon at La Guerite

  • La Guérite, Cannes
  • TUESDAY 20 JUNE 2023
RSVP

JOIN US

Join us for an intimate culinary experience at La Guerite, a stunning restaurant nestled on the picturesque rocky cove of Sainte-Marguerite island. Enjoy the freshest Mediterranean cuisine and breathtaking panoramic views of the Bay of Cannes.

Don’t miss this exclusive opportunity to escape the Cannes Festival bustle and immerse yourself in the beauty and serenity of the French Riviera with your professional peers.

First seating service from 12pm until 3pm

Complimentary speedboat shuttle service included at 11:45am

Meet at the Pickup Location promptly at 11:45 - Port Pierre Canto, Harbor

The pick-up is made from the Harbor Offices on the right side jetty (in front of the Báoli Club)
